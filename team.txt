History
The Louisville RiverFrogs were a professional ice hockey team competing
 in the East Coast Hockey League (ECHL), which was a mid-level
  professional American hockey league with teams from all over
  the United States as well as one franchise from Canada.
   The team was based in Louisville, Kentucky and played from 1995 to 1998.
    Their home venue was Broadbent Arena 
    (nicknamed "The Swamp" for their duration; capacity 6,600)
     at the Kentucky Exposition Center.
